About East Pinnacle

East Pinnacle is accessed by the Indian Fort Mountain Trail located the Berea College Forest. The trail system is located just outside of Berea city limits, making it a convenient and popular place to hike. This area has archaeological significance as it was once home of the ancient Adena culture.

Pillars extracted from the Domestic and Antarctic Names (State and Topical Gazetteer) published by the United States Board on Geographic Namkes. (geonames.usgs.gov) Data accessed September, 2018. Contours were extracted from a USGS NED 1/3 arc-second raster digital elevation model. This data can be accessed at the USGS 3D Elevation Program (usgs.gov)

Tools used in this project

  • PostGIS was used to store/query the national dataset.
  • Data is shown on Mapbox web map with geolocation services enabled. If the map got popular it would require a fee to use.
  • GDAL and QGIS 3.2 to convert raster elevation units and create contours.
